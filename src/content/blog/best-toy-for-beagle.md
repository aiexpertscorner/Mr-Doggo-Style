## Beagle-Specific Toy Tips
- Needs 60-90 minutes active exercise daily; thrives with a job or sport.
- Seasonal shedder — daily brushing during spring and fall coat blows is essential.
- Brush 2-3× weekly to maintain coat condition and reduce shedding around the home.

## Frequently Asked Questions
### What toys are best for Beagles?
Beagles thrive with high-energy toys like fetch balls, tug ropes, and puzzle feeders. The most important factor is matching toy durability to your dog's chewing intensity — heavy-duty rubber like KONG Extreme is recommended for this breed.
### How often should I give my Beagle new toys?
Rotate toys every 3–5 days to maintain novelty and engagement. Beagles benefit from the mental stimulation of encountering "new" toys regularly. Keep 4–6 toys active and rotate them.
### Are squeaky toys safe for Beagles?
Squeaky toys are generally safe under supervision, but not for unsupervised chewing — the squeaker is a swallowing hazard once the toy is open. For this high-energy breed, plush squeaky toys are best used as interactive play toys, not chew toys.
