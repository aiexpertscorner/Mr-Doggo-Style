## Border Collie-Specific Grooming Tool Tips

- Requires 90+ minutes vigorous exercise daily — fetch, agility, or long hikes.
- Seasonal shedder — daily brushing during spring and fall coat blows is essential.
- Brush 2-3× weekly to maintain coat condition and reduce shedding around the home.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How often should I groom my Border Collie?

Border Collies are heavy shedders that need brushing 3–4 times per week minimum. During spring and fall coat blows, daily brushing prevents mats and significantly reduces shedding around your home.

### What grooming tools does a Border Collie need?

Essential tools for Border Collies: a deShedding tool (FURminator is the gold standard), a slicker brush for regular maintenance, a metal comb for tangle detection, and nail clippers. Professional grooming every 6–8 weeks rounds out the routine.

### Do Border Collies shed a lot?

Yes — Border Collies are considered heavy shedders. Regular deShedding with a tool like the FURminator can reduce shedding by up to 90%. Expect more shedding in spring and fall during seasonal coat changes.
