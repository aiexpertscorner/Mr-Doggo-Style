## What Fresh Feeding Can Support

Dogs eating a nutritionally complete fresh diet — whether home-cooked or subscription-delivered — often show improvements in:

- **Coat quality** — fat-soluble vitamins from whole food sources
- **Digestive regularity** — higher moisture content and fewer fillers
- **Weight management** — easier calorie calibration with whole ingredients
- **Food sensitivity management** — full visibility into every ingredient

These are potential benefits, not guarantees. Always transition gradually, and consult your vet when changing a dog's diet significantly.
