## Frequently Asked Questions
### What toys are best for Chesapeake Bay Retrievers?
Chesapeake Bay Retrievers thrive with high-energy toys like fetch balls, tug ropes, and puzzle feeders. The most important factor is matching toy durability to your dog's chewing intensity — heavy-duty rubber like KONG Extreme is recommended for this breed.
### How often should I give my Chesapeake Bay Retriever new toys?
Rotate toys every 3–5 days to maintain novelty and engagement. Chesapeake Bay Retrievers benefit from the mental stimulation of encountering "new" toys regularly. Keep 4–6 toys active and rotate them.
### Are squeaky toys safe for Chesapeake Bay Retrievers?
Squeaky toys are generally safe under supervision, but not for unsupervised chewing — the squeaker is a swallowing hazard once the toy is open. For this high-energy breed, plush squeaky toys are best used as interactive play toys, not chew toys.
