- Benefits from 30-60 minutes daily exercise — walks, play sessions, or swimming.

- Brush 2-3× weekly to maintain coat condition and reduce shedding around the home.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How often should I groom my Pomeranian?

Pomeranians are heavy shedders that need brushing 3–4 times per week minimum. During spring and fall coat blows, daily brushing prevents mats and significantly reduces shedding around your home.

### What grooming tools does a Pomeranian need?

Essential tools for Pomeranians: a deShedding tool (FURminator is the gold standard), a slicker brush for regular maintenance, a metal comb for tangle detection, and nail clippers. Professional grooming every 6–8 weeks rounds out the routine.

### Do Pomeranians shed a lot?

Yes — Pomeranians are considered heavy shedders. Regular deShedding with a tool like the FURminator can reduce shedding by up to 90%. Expect more shedding in spring and fall during seasonal coat changes.
