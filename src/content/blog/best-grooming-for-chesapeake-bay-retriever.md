## Frequently Asked Questions

### How often should I groom my Chesapeake Bay Retriever?

Chesapeake Bay Retrievers are heavy shedders that need brushing 3–4 times per week minimum. During spring and fall coat blows, daily brushing prevents mats and significantly reduces shedding around your home.

### What grooming tools does a Chesapeake Bay Retriever need?

Essential tools for Chesapeake Bay Retrievers: a deShedding tool (FURminator is the gold standard), a slicker brush for regular maintenance, a metal comb for tangle detection, and nail clippers. Professional grooming every 6–8 weeks rounds out the routine.

### Do Chesapeake Bay Retrievers shed a lot?

Yes — Chesapeake Bay Retrievers are considered heavy shedders. Regular deShedding with a tool like the FURminator can reduce shedding by up to 90%. Expect more shedding in spring and fall during seasonal coat changes.
