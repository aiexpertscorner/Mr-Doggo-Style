## Frequently Asked Questions
### What is the best dog food for German Shepherd Dogs?
For German Shepherd Dogs, we recommend Purina Pro Plan Large Breed Adult. Large breed formulas with controlled calcium (1.0–1.8% DM) and glucosamine are essential for this breed. Always transition foods gradually over 7–10 days.
### How much should I feed my German Shepherd Dog?
A typical adult German Shepherd Dog weighing 50–90 lbs needs 3–4 cups of dry food per day, split into two meals. Use the bag's guidelines as a starting point and adjust based on body condition score. You should be able to feel but not see the ribs.
### Do German Shepherd Dogs have food allergies?
German Shepherd Dogs can develop food sensitivities, most commonly to proteins (chicken, beef) rather than grains. If you notice itching, ear infections, or digestive issues, discuss an elimination diet with your vet. Novel protein formulas (salmon, duck, venison) work well for sensitive German Shepherd Dogs.
### Is grain-free food good for German Shepherd Dogs?
Unless your German Shepherd Dog has a confirmed grain allergy, grain-free is not necessary and may carry additional cardiac risk (FDA DCM investigation). High-quality grain-inclusive formulas from established brands like Purina Pro Plan and Hill's are the vet-recommended choice.
