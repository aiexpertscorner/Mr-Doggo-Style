## Frequently Asked Questions

### What size bed does a Cavalier King Charles Spaniel need?

Cavalier King Charles Spaniels typically need a Small (24") or Medium (30") bed. Measure your dog from nose to tail and add 6–8 inches for comfortable stretching room. When in doubt, size up.

### Do Cavalier King Charles Spaniels need an orthopedic bed?

Cavalier King Charles Spaniels benefit from foam support, especially as they age. Any dog with joint issues needs orthopedic support, and preventive use from age 5+ is recommended by most veterinarians.

### How often should I wash my Cavalier King Charles Spaniel's bed?

Wash the cover every 1–2 weeks, or immediately after accidents. Spot-clean the foam insert with mild soap and allow to fully air dry — never machine wash foam. A waterproof inner liner (like Friends Forever includes) makes this process much easier.
