## Frequently Asked Questions

### What toys are best for Cavalier King Charles Spaniels?

Cavalier King Charles Spaniels enjoy moderate play with durable chew toys and interactive puzzles. The most important factor is matching toy durability to your dog's chewing intensity — standard rubber toys are usually sufficient.

### How often should I give my Cavalier King Charles Spaniel new toys?

Rotate toys every 3–5 days to maintain novelty and engagement. Cavalier King Charles Spaniels are smart dogs who quickly lose interest in familiar toys — rotation is especially important for this breed. Keep 4–6 toys active and rotate them.

### Are squeaky toys safe for Cavalier King Charles Spaniels?

Squeaky toys are generally safe under supervision, but not for unsupervised chewing — the squeaker is a swallowing hazard once the toy is open. Monitor your dog during play and remove damaged toys immediately.
