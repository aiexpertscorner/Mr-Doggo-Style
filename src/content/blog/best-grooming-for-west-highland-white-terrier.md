- Benefits from 30-60 minutes daily exercise — walks, play sessions, or swimming.

- Coat requires daily brushing to prevent matting; professional grooming every 6-8 weeks.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How often should I groom my West Highland White Terrier?

West Highland White Terriers are heavy shedders that need brushing 3–4 times per week minimum. During spring and fall coat blows, daily brushing prevents mats and significantly reduces shedding around your home.

### What grooming tools does a West Highland White Terrier need?

Essential tools for West Highland White Terriers: a deShedding tool (FURminator is the gold standard), a slicker brush for regular maintenance, a metal comb for tangle detection, and nail clippers. Professional grooming every 6–8 weeks rounds out the routine.

### Do West Highland White Terriers shed a lot?

Yes — West Highland White Terriers are considered heavy shedders. Regular deShedding with a tool like the FURminator can reduce shedding by up to 90%. Expect more shedding in spring and fall during seasonal coat changes.
