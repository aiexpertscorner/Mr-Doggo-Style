## Frequently Asked Questions
### What is the best dog food for Cane Corsos?
For Cane Corsos, we recommend Taste of the Wild High Prairie. Look for a formula with high-quality animal protein as the first ingredient and an AAFCO feeding trial statement. Always transition foods gradually over 7–10 days.
### How much should I feed my Cane Corso?
A typical adult Cane Corso weighing 50–80 lbs needs 1.5–2.5 cups per day depending on activity level. Use the bag's guidelines as a starting point and adjust based on body condition score. You should be able to feel but not see the ribs.
### Do Cane Corsos have food allergies?
Cane Corsos can develop food sensitivities, most commonly to proteins (chicken, beef) rather than grains. If you notice itching, ear infections, or digestive issues, discuss an elimination diet with your vet. Novel protein formulas (salmon, duck, venison) work well for sensitive Cane Corsos.
### Is grain-free food good for Cane Corsos?
Unless your Cane Corso has a confirmed grain allergy, grain-free is not necessary and may carry additional cardiac risk (FDA DCM investigation). High-quality grain-inclusive formulas from established brands like Purina Pro Plan and Hill's are the vet-recommended choice.
