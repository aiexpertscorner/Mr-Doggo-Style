## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the best dog food for Rhodesian Ridgebacks?

For Rhodesian Ridgebacks, we recommend Purina Pro Plan Large Breed Adult. Large breed formulas with controlled calcium (1.0–1.8% DM) and glucosamine are essential for this breed. Always transition foods gradually over 7–10 days.

### How much should I feed my Rhodesian Ridgeback?

A typical adult Rhodesian Ridgeback weighing 70–85 lbs needs 3–4 cups of dry food per day, split into two meals. Use the bag's guidelines as a starting point and adjust based on body condition score. You should be able to feel but not see the ribs.

### Do Rhodesian Ridgebacks have food allergies?

Rhodesian Ridgebacks can develop food sensitivities, most commonly to proteins (chicken, beef) rather than grains. If you notice itching, ear infections, or digestive issues, discuss an elimination diet with your vet. Novel protein formulas (salmon, duck, venison) work well for sensitive Rhodesian Ridgebacks.

### Is grain-free food good for Rhodesian Ridgebacks?

Unless your Rhodesian Ridgeback has a confirmed grain allergy, grain-free is not necessary and may carry additional cardiac risk (FDA DCM investigation). High-quality grain-inclusive formulas from established brands like Purina Pro Plan and Hill's are the vet-recommended choice.
