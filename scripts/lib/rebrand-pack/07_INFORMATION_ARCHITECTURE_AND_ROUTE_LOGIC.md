# INFORMATION ARCHITECTURE AND ROUTE LOGIC

Refine the site's IA without reckless route churn.

## Known likely route families
- `/blog/*`
- `/breeds/*`
- `/dog-names/*`
- `/cost-calculator/*`
- `/categories/*`

## Goal
Strengthen the relationship between these route families.

## Suggested logic to evaluate
### Breeds
Breed pages should connect to:
- fit/lifestyle guidance
- cost/planning
- food
- training
- grooming
- health
- names
- products

### Dog names
Names pages should connect naturally from:
- breed pages
- puppy content
- first-time owner content

### Cost calculators
Cost pages should connect from:
- breed pages
- getting-a-dog content
- puppy basics
- insurance/service comparison content

### Categories
Category hubs should not feel isolated.
They should link to:
- core evergreen guides
- comparisons
- breed-relevant pages
- planning/care content

### Blog
Blog pages should support hub strength and not remain a loose archive.

## Rule
Prefer better hub labeling, cross-linking, and template logic before changing URL structure.
