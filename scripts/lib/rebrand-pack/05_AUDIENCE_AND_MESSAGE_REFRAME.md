# AUDIENCE AND MESSAGE REFRAME

Before rewriting the homepage and hubs, frame PupWiki correctly.

## Important
PupWiki is broader than “dog owner tips”.

It should support two major intent families:

### A. Before getting a dog
- should I get a dog?
- which breed fits me?
- what does a dog cost?
- apartment/family/kids/allergy/beginner fit
- what do I need before bringing a dog home?

### B. After getting a dog
- food and nutrition
- behavior and training
- grooming and care
- health and wellness
- products, gear, services, insurance

## Messaging position
PupWiki should feel like:
- a practical dog knowledge guide
- a trustworthy decision-support brand
- a breed research and care resource
- a cleaner editorial destination, not a random pet blog

## Required outputs
Create:
- one core positioning statement
- one homepage mission statement
- three supporting message pillars
- one short brand description
- one longer about-style description
